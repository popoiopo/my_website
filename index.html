Welcome on my personal website!

Hey! My name is Bas Châtel, and you must be here to check out my personal site! This site is made mainly to let you get to know me a bit. What I’ve been up to lately etc. So first let me tell you a little bit about myself.

I am a 25 year old and just finished his bachelor in Psychobiology and a minor in Programming at the University of Amsterdam. I know, I know.. Quite a big change to go from studying the brain (from a molecular to psychological level) to begin learning how to program stuff. Or at least that’s how it felt to me in the beginning. But programming is becoming an increasingly important skill (with all the big data, systems neuroscience and what not) in science because it's quite handy for visualising and analysing just about anything you want! That’s one of the main reasons I enrolled in the Computational Science Master (pretty excited about that one to be honest). If you want to know more about these educational programs I’ve been following, just click on those links and it will take you to everything you need to know!

As you can see underneath this section there are several pictures linking to various places in this website. I particularly recommend the tutorials section! It's become quite a hobby for me to write these, and they're actually being used at the Metis Montessori High School! Furthermore there are projects ranging from my bachelor thesis to all kinds of programming assignments/projects. This will grow over time and more projects will be added! Please take a look around and try some stuff out. If you enjoy it, have some questions, pointers for improvement or suggestions (maybe for a next project or something) you can always contact me via the contact form at at the contact page, and I will answer as soon as possible. Have fun! (oh and if you want to have a peek at the codes from the projects, check my Github!)

Lets start learning!
