About Me

Bas Châtel - Assistent Information Managment / TA Montessori / TA Programming Minor UvA

Last year I've been quite busy with learning stuff about programming, then teaching people how to do it and just enjoying my gap year between studying PsychoBiology en Computational Science.

Everybody's got hobbies. And I am no exception! As you might have allready seen, I've dedicated my bachelors thesis to studying the effect of a kickboxing match on cognitive ability and the reason I got this gig was because I've been kickboxing for a while now myself (about 9 years now). I even fought with a fair share of match fighters during training sessions! Furthermore I've done a bit of MMA for about a year, and about 7 years of boxing (mind that these overlap!).

Who doesn't love a good party, festival or just your run of the mill chill at the park in the sunshine?! Just love it when all these activities out of winters frosty fingers.
